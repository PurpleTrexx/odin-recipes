Homemade Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese

Come smell the aroma of melting cheese and joy!

This homemade Mac and Cheese will bring in friends, family and maybe even enemies. We have cultivated a cheesey and amazing combination for all to love.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Boil noodles until aldente
  2. Melt cheese and butter into a mixing bowl.
  3. Combine noodles, cheese, butter and milk.
  4. Enjoy
