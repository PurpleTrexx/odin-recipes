Scalloped Potatoes

Bring the family together for a midwestern treat!

A midwestern recipe that has been used for generations this Scalloped Potato dish combines amazing flavors with \ a taste of history.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Boil potatoes
  2. Melt cheese and butter into a mixing bowl.
  3. Slice Potatoes into thin slices.
  4. Combine potatoes, cheese, milk, and salt and pepper.
  5. Bake at 450 for 20 minutes.
  6. Enjoy
