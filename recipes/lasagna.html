World's Best Lasagna

Grandmas around the country would be jealous of this family recipe

The world's best Lasagna combines a combination of traditional ingredients to give you that homestyle italian taste.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Combine meat, tomatoes and seasonings in pan for sauce
  2. Boil Noodles until al dente
  3. Layer noodles, cheese, and meat sauce
  4. Bake at 450 for 4 hours
  5. Let sit for 20-30 minutes
  6. Enjoy
